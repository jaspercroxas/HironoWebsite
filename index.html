About Hirono

Created by contemporary artist Lang, Hirono is a complex character. Through Hirono, Lang aims to highlight the subtle fluctuations of life, its ups and downs, and attempts to freeze in time the elusive feelings that reveal our true character - love, joy, sadness, fear, kindness, cowardice and others.
